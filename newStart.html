What is the sale about?

   
Giving out a a great diversity of seeds is a major goal of ours. As a family, we've been growing plants for the past 10 years. This action conveys that we've been preserving seeds, year by year, monitering its health, looks, and keeping them away from pesticides and preservatives. Our wish is to provide seeds to famillies interested in gardening in the Marion, Cedar Rapids, and Robins area.













How would the sale work?

Attached to this website is a pdf of the available seeds/plants for this gardening season. By calling our main phone number : 319-573-1798, you will be able to contact us and order the seeds that you request for.
One order of a particular plant or seed would be one seed packet. One seed packet is 6-10 seeds.
